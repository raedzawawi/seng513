Spring Rolls

Ingredients
# Ingredient Quantity
1 Black Pepper 2 tbsp
2 Chicken 500g
3 Red chilli powder 2tbsp
4 Salt 2 tbsp
5 Soy Sauce 2 tbsp
6 Spring onions 4
Directions
  • Cut all the vegetables up
  • Shred the chicken
  • Add soy sauce
  • Mix everything and refrigerate.
  • Fill that stuff into the spring roll sheets. Pretty easy stuff.
  • Fry 'em and eat them in whatever way you like.
