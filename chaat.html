Chaat

Ingredients
# Ingredient Quantity
1 Black Pepper 2 tbsp
2 Chickpeas 500g
3 Red chilli powder 2tbsp
4 Salt 2 tbsp
5 Yoghurt 250g
6 Onions 250g
7 Chillies 5
8 Lemon juice 2 tbsp
Directions
  • Dice the onions and the chillies.
  • Cook chickpeas with the onions and lemon juice
  • Serve with Yoghurt and spices.
  • Eat!
