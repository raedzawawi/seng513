Samosas

Ingredients
# Ingredient Quantity
1 Black Pepper 2 tbsp
2 Beef 500g
3 Red chilli powder 2tbsp
4 Salt 2 tbsp
5 Onions 2
Directions
  • Cut stuff up and make sure you cut stuff up nicely.
  • Mix stuff up in a certain way that it stays mixed.
  • Fill that stuff into the samosa sheets. Pretty easy stuff.
  • Fry 'em and eat them in whatever way you like.
